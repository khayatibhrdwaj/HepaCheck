Information Hub

Understand the meaning of common liver-related non-invasive scores and what they indicate.

FIB-4

FIB-4 combines age, AST, ALT, and platelet count to estimate the likelihood of advanced fibrosis risk in a non-invasive way.

APRI

APRI uses AST and platelet count to assess fibrosis-related risk signals and is often used as a quick screening indicator.

NFS

The NAFLD Fibrosis Score includes age, BMI, diabetes status, AST/ALT, platelets, and albumin to estimate fibrosis probability.

HOMA-IR

HOMA-IR estimates insulin resistance using fasting glucose and fasting insulin, giving a metabolic view alongside liver risk indicators.
