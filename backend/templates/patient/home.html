NAFLD Education Centre

Learn about fatty liver disease, its progression, and how to manage it

🧬What is NAFLD?▼
Nonalcoholic Fatty Liver Disease (NAFLD) is a condition where excess fat builds up in the liver in people who drink little to no alcohol. It affects up to 25% of the global population.
📈How NAFLD Progresses▼
NAFLD may progress to NASH → Fibrosis → Cirrhosis → Liver failure if left unmanaged.
🤔What Causes It?▼
Key risk factors: obesity, type 2 diabetes, insulin resistance, high cholesterol, and metabolic syndrome.
💊How to Manage NAFLD▼
Losing 7–10% of body weight through diet and exercise significantly reduces liver fat. A Mediterranean diet and 150+ min/week aerobic activity are recommended.
🔬Understanding Your Scores▼
